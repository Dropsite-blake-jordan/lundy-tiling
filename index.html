About Lundy Tiling

Regan Lundy —
The Tiler Who
Gets It Right

Newcastle · Lake Macquarie · Hunter Valley

Lundy Tiling is a family business servicing Newcastle, Lake Macquarie and The Hunter Valley. The trade has been passed down through the generations — first started by Regan's dad, and carried on today by Regan and his team, who take pride in delivering work that's precise, professional, and built to last.

Regan is a fully licensed and insured tiler, and every job — whether it's a bathroom renovation, a new kitchen splashback, or a full floor installation — gets the same level of care and attention. No shortcuts. No excuses. Just quality tiling done right.
